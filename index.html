Projects

The Arduino board is like a little wizard. When the world gets dark, it waves its wand and a green LED light appears. But when the sun says hello, it makes the light vanish. This special trick makes a streetlight that works at night and rests during the day. It's a smart way to help in places without electricity. The Arduino streetlight is a simple, but clever, project that's pretty amazing!
